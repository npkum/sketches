Powerful owl (Ninox strenua), is the largest of Australia’s owls. The male’s deep, low and impressive ‘whoo-hoo’ call may be heard over 2 km away. Powerful owls mate for life – with a pair bond that can last for over 30 years.

Barking owl (Ninox connivens),are named after their distinctive double note ‘woof woof’ calls, which may be a duet between pairs.

Southern boobook (Ninox boobook ),is the smallest and most common owl in Australia. Their repetitive two-note call 'boo-book'.
